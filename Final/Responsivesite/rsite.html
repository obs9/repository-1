There are many benefits to owning a pet. Including lowering blood pressure, social interaction, and the opportunity to form bonds. When I heard the Miami Dade Animal Adoption services were giving away cats for free, I knew it was time for me to save a furry animal and bring it into my home. 

However, the volunteers failed to mention some of the behaviours of a kitten that can take some getting used to. I have created a list:

What I didn't know What I know now
Cats a cheaper than dogs to feed, but have trickier diets so table leftovers isn't an option. Cats will ruin anything you remotely care about.
Cats are bi-turnal and will wake you up in the wee hours of the morning to be fed. Once you get a cat, you're going to want another one.
Cats most often kneed and scratch things when they need their claws trimmed. Cats will always fight you on this. Cats are easier to sneak on flights for free than dogs.
Cats don't need toys. Just give them a paper box or a plastic bag or some string and they'll go crazy. Cats will try to sleep in any small space including the dryer.
